Academics

At VIT University, we offer a wide range of academic programs to cater to the diverse interests and career aspirations of our students. Explore our courses below:

  • Computer Science

    Prepare for a career in software development, data science, and more with our Computer Science program.

  • Electrical Engineering

    Learn the fundamentals of electrical systems, electronics, and power generation in our Electrical Engineering program.

  • Mechanical Engineering

    Explore the world of machines, design, and manufacturing with our Mechanical Engineering program.

  • Civil Engineering

    Build the infrastructure of the future with our Civil Engineering program, covering structural and environmental engineering.

  • Chemical Engineering

    Discover the processes and techniques behind chemical manufacturing and innovation in our Chemical Engineering program.

  • Physics

    Delve into the mysteries of the universe and the fundamental laws of nature with our Physics program.

  • Mathematics

    Develop strong mathematical and analytical skills that are essential in various fields with our Mathematics program.

  • Business Administration

    Prepare for leadership roles in the corporate world with our Business Administration program.

  • Psychology

    Explore the human mind and behavior in our Psychology program, and gain insights into mental health and well-being.

  • English Literature

    Immerse yourself in the world of literature, language, and culture with our English Literature program.
